Since 1949

It started with a single glove wall.

When the doors first opened in South Minneapolis, St. Mane's was one room: a wall of gloves, a rack of bats, and a founder who could size a kid for cleats by eye. Word got around that the advice was honest and the laces were always in stock.

Seventy-seven years later we've grown into the area's go-to for new and pre-owned gear across every season — but we still size cleats by hand, and we still tell you when the cheaper one is the better buy.

"We refuse to sell you something we wouldn't put on our own bench."

A few innings worth remembering.

1949

Founder opens a one-room sporting goods shop in South Minneapolis, stocking gloves, bats, and cleats for local little leagues.

1968

Adds a screen-printing press out back — the first season the whole town's teams wore matching jerseys.

1985

Second generation takes the counter and launches buy-sell-trade, putting quality used gear in reach for every family.

2003

Expands into the storefront next door — adding hockey, golf, and a dedicated team-uniform room.

2026

Third generation brings St. Mane's online: browse the floor, reserve for pickup, and trade in from home.
